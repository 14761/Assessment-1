Wine is an alcoholic beverage made from fermented grapes or other fruits. The basic process of winemaking involves crushing the fruit (usually grapes) to extract the juice, fermenting the juice with yeast to convert sugars into alcohol, and aging the resulting liquid to develop its flavors and characteristics.
